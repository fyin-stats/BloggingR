I came across a video titled “LinkedIn CEO Shares Advice On Leadership, Hiring and Firing” on YouTube. I found the first part of Jeff Weiner’s interview was full of insightful quotes about life in general, and I’d like to take notes here.

Five keys to happiness

  • To live in the moment
  • To be a spectator of your own thoughts, especially when you become emotional
  • Better to be loving rather than to be right
  • To be grateful for at least one thing everyday
  • To be of service to others and help others every chance you get

Compassion and wisdom

Wisdom without compassion is ruthless, compassion without wisdom is folly.
